Home Is Where The Heart Is By

🏠 💖

William Worthless

More About The Poet Inspo Website Example

Home is where the heart is so the people say
home is where the love is. it's there every day.
It's your little castle that belongs to you
there to last a lifetime for your whole life through.
A place to call your own with lots of love to share
wherever there's a home your heart is always there.
Happy and content you always be
true love every day you will always see.
